ETO Eastern Conference 2026: Interest Registration Is Open

KBI Hong Kong hosts the Emerging Technologies Olympiad Eastern Conference — a global, cross-disciplinary conference (BCTT, DSAI, QCIT) at the Canadian International School of Hong Kong, 9–11 October 2026.

ETO is distinct from the I2OL subject olympiads (IBCOL, IDSOL, IQCOL). Students worldwide are invited to attend in person; interest registration is open now.

About KBI HK

KBI Hong Kong is the regional chapter of the Königsberger Bridges Institute, established in 2016. It is a non-profit, youth-run organisation focused on emerging-technology education.

Key Facts
  • ·Established 2016 — longest-running KBI chapter globally
  • ·1,000+ community members across HK
  • ·3 core technology tracks: Blockchain, Data Science, Quantum
  • ·50+ programmes and events delivered
