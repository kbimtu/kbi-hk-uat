About KBI Hong Kong

Who We Are

KBI Hong Kong is part of the Königsberger Bridges Institute (KBI), an international nonprofit named after the Königsberg Bridges Problem — the mathematical puzzle that Euler solved in 1736 by inventing an entirely new field. We've been doing the same with students ever since.

We bring together students, educators, researchers, and industry leaders to do what most institutions don't: connect rigorous thinking with real-world application, from day one.

Our Story
Our Work

Compete. Learn. Build. Connect.

Four pillars. One community. Designed for students who want more than just a classroom.

Compete

Olympiad-track competitions in blockchain, data science, and quantum computing — plus ETO, our global emerging-technologies conference.

Learn

Build

Applied research, working groups, and collaborative projects with real outputs.

Connect

A peer community, mentorship network, and direct pathways to institutions and industry.

Who We Serve

Where Do You Fit?

Whether you're a student ready to compete, an institution looking to partner, or an employer who wants access to Hong Kong's most motivated emerging-tech talent — there's a place for you here.

For Students

You don't need to be a coder. You need to be curious, collaborative, and willing to tackle real problems.

KBI Hong Kong gives students structured, credible pathways into emerging technologies — whether you're a secondary school student just starting out, or a university student ready to compete on an international stage.

International Competition

Compete in IBCOL, IDSOL, IQCOL, and ETO — internationally recognised olympiad pathways.

Certifications & Training

Earn ISFRETIC certifications in blockchain, data science, and quantum computing

Mentorship & Community

Access to a 1,000+ member network of peers, alumni mentors, and industry professionals.

University & Career Pathways

Competition records, certifications, and KBI recognition strengthen university applications and CV profiles.

For Educators & Academic Institutions

Give your students access to something the syllabus can't.

KBI Hong Kong extends learning beyond the classroom — connecting your students to international competition stages, hands-on training, and a community of peers and mentors across Hong Kong and beyond.

Curriculum-Adjacent Programmes

Competitions and training that complement STEM, computer science, and business curricula at secondary and tertiary level.

Institutional Partnerships

Formal academic partnerships with KBI HK including co-branding, event hosting, and research collaboration.

Teacher Development

Professional development resources, workshops, and community access for educators entering these fields.

Student Achievement Recognition

Official KBI certificates and competition results that schools can include in programme records and reporting.

For Professionals

Give back. Find talent. Shape the field.

You know what the industry actually needs. We have the students who want to learn it. Whether you mentor, judge, speak, or collaborate — the community benefits, and so do you.

Mentorship Programme

Mentor competition participants and training cohorts. Direct, meaningful contribution to youth development in your field.

Speaking & Judging

Contribute as a guest speaker, panel judge at ETO Eastern Conference 2026, or workshop facilitator to our training programmes.

Research Collaboration

Collaborate with KBI HK research groups on applied technology projects bridging academia and industry.

Talent Pipeline

Early access to competition finalists and certification graduates for internship and graduate recruitment opportunities.

For Sponsors & Donors

Support Hong Kong's next generation of technology leaders.

Your sponsorship funds serious, rigorous programmes that directly develop the next cohort of Hong Kong's emerging-tech workforce — with brand visibility, talent access, and mission alignment.

Brand Visibility

Logo placement, event naming rights, co-branded media, and programme recognition across all KBI HK channels.

Direct Talent Access

Finalist introductions, talent introductions, and recruitment pathways to KBI HK's most accomplished participants.

Non-Profit Impact

Direct contribution to a registered non-profit delivering structured education that genuinely helps students and Hong Kong's tech sector.

Flexible Tiers

Bronze, Silver, Gold sponsorship tiers and custom institutional partnership arrangements to match your goals and budget.
