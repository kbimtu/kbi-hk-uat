Personal Information Collection Statement (PICS)

This is a personal information collection statement under the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance ("the Ordinance").

  1. Purpose of Collection

    The personal data provided by you to Kingsbridge Institute Limited, also known as Königsberger Bridges Institute ("KBI") during application for a programme or an initiative ("programme"), (if admitted as participant) during programme registration, throughout your programme and upon leaving KBI will be used by KBI for the following purposes:

    During application for programme

    • As basis for selection for application of a programme;
    • Managing your application account and processing your application with KBI;
    • Identifying possible multiple applications and obtaining records of your existing and previous studies and activities at KBI;
    • Verifying your identity, country of origin, country of residency and academic records;
    • Conducting statistical analysis, research, surveys, quality assurance and review;
    • Facilitating communications between you and KBI;
    • Other purposes directly relating to any of the above.

    Upon your admission as a student of KBI, your personal data provided during application will become part of your official account records. In case your application is not accepted, your personal data provided during application will be disposed of according to the prevailing policy of KBI.

    During and upon leaving a KBI programme

    • Processing your application for competition, activities, services and facilities provided by unit, teams and directorates of KBI or organizations associated with KBI;
    • Processing the application and arrangement for programmes inter alia to competition ,student visa, student exchange programmes, study tours, placement, internship, Service Learning & Work Integrated Education, training and scholarships, etc.
    • Managing alumni and community affairs;
    • Verifying application status, qualifications and activity records;
    • Disclosing information about your awards and achievements;
    • Conducting statistical analysis, research, surveys, quality assurance and review;
    • Facilitating communications between you and KBI;
    • Other purposes directly relating to any of the above.

    In case of application for admission to a programme jointly offered or collaborated by KBI and its associated organization(s), your personal data will be transferred to the partner organization(s) for the above purposes.

  2. Obligation to Provide Personal Data

    You are required to provide your personal data, other than those items indicated as optional. In the absence of your personal data, we may not be able to process your application for programme, provide you with the requested activities, services or facilities or maintain contact with you.

  3. Disclosure and Transfer of Personal Data

    KBI will keep your personal data confidential and only authorized staff members of KBI will have access to and handle your personal data. KBI may disclose or transfer your personal data to the following parties within or outside Hong Kong solely for the purposes set out in paragraph 1 above.

    • KBI's chapters and related alumni ;
    • Agencies, service providers, and contractors engaged in activities on behalf of KBI;
    • Other institutions and government authorities in Hong Kong or other places for verification of qualifications and prizes you obtained from KBI.

    KBI may disclose your personal data when authorized or required by law and in response to requests from law enforcement agencies, government departments or regulatory authorities.

  4. Access and Correction of Personal Data

    You have the right to request for access and correction of your personal data held by KBI. A fee may be imposed by KBI for processing your data access request.

    During application period, programme period and post-programme period, correction of personal data can be performed via email to Talent Management Office at talent@kb.institute. Any data access and correction request according to the Ordinance should be made by completing the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance Data Access Request Form and sending it to the KBI Data Protection Officer at dpo@kb.institute.

  5. Privacy Policy Statement

    You may click here for the Privacy Policy Statement of KBI.
