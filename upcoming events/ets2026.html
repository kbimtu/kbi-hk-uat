About ETS 2026

The 2026 SkillSprint edition

SkillSprint is KBI HK's series of short, intensive training editions. Each year focuses on a defined theme. In 2025, the series ran as FinTech SkillSprint at City University of Hong Kong. In 2026, the edition is Emerging-Technology SkillSprint (ETS) — broadening the focus to emerging technologies across blockchain, data workflows, AI practice, and related fields.

ETS is designed for learners who want practical exposure without a full semester-long commitment: serious content, accessible delivery, and sessions shaped around applied understanding rather than hype.

What to Expect

Concentrated workshops, real domains

  • ·Short, focused sessions across multiple emerging-technology domains
  • ·Practical orientation — concepts connected to how work is actually done
  • ·Part of the wider KBI training pathway alongside ETTI, ABC5, and competition programmes
  • ·Recognition in KBI membership records for participants who complete sessions
