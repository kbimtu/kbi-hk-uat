Training Programme

Emerging Tech Training
& Internship

ETTI is where emerging-technology learning moves beyond the lecture. Through partner-based training, mentorship, and supervised practical exposure, students begin to see how real work happens across blockchain, data, AI, and related fields.

Each edition is developed with a host institution, so the programme is shaped around a real cohort, a real context, and a real educational purpose.

About the Programme

Training that meets the real world

Some students learn quickly in the classroom but still have little sense of how technical work is actually done. ETTI is designed to close that gap.

It combines structured teaching with guided exposure to working environments, so participants do not only study concepts, but also encounter the pace, expectations, and judgement involved in applying them.

Rather than delivering the same format every time, KBI HK works with each host to shape the edition around the students, calendar, and level of readiness involved.

What Makes ETTI Different

More than a short course

  • ·It is built with a host institution, not delivered as a generic standalone class.
  • ·It combines taught content with supervised practical or ecosystem exposure.
  • ·It focuses on how emerging-technology work is practised, not only how it is explained in theory.
  • ·It can prepare students for deeper participation in KBI programmes, projects, and future certification pathways.
How It Works

A typical ETTI pathway

Each edition is different, but most ETTI pathways follow the same broad shape.

1

Partner design

KBI HK works with the host institution to understand the cohort, align expectations, and shape a programme that fits the academic context and time available.

2

Structured learning

Students take part in focused modules that may cover topics such as cryptography, consensus, distributed systems, data workflows, AI practice, or quantum fundamentals, depending on the edition.

3

Practical exposure

Where appropriate, participants are given supervised exposure to a lab, innovation environment, partner organisation, working group, or internship-style setting that helps them understand how the field operates in practice.

4

Completion and next steps

Students complete the edition with a clearer sense of the field, stronger technical context, and a better understanding of where they may want to go next.

Editions

Each edition has a host

ETTI is not one fixed template. It is a programme model that takes a slightly different shape depending on the institution, the cohort, and the learning goals involved.

LETTI — Lingnan University

The 2024 Lingnan edition introduced students to emerging technologies, cryptography fundamentals, and consensus concepts through structured online learning and interactive activities. It was designed to make serious ideas accessible without making them simplistic.

School Internship — DGJS at HKSTP

In 2025, students from DGJS joined a short blockchain-focused internship experience at Hong Kong Science and Technology Parks. The value of the edition was not only in the content taught, but in letting students encounter the technology ecosystem more directly.

Future editions

New editions are developed when the right host partner, cohort, and delivery context are in place. Some may be university-based; others may be designed for schools or special partner settings.

Certification

Training first, certification later

ETTI is a training programme, not a formal assessment. Its purpose is to prepare students through guided learning and practical exposure, not to certify competence by itself.

Formal credentials for demonstrated competence sit separately within KBI's broader certification pathway. For some participants, ETTI may become part of the preparation that helps them approach those opportunities with greater confidence when available. Learn more about ISFRETIC.

Why It Matters

Why this programme exists

Emerging technology is often taught either too abstractly or too superficially. ETTI is built on the belief that students deserve something better: education that is serious, contextual, and connected to the environments in which real work happens.

The goal is not to imitate industry for appearances. It is to help students develop judgement, orientation, and confidence early enough that future opportunities feel reachable rather than distant.

Partner or Participate

Bring ETTI to your institution

ETTI works best when it is built around a real partner and a real educational need. If your school, university, or institution is interested in running an edition, KBI HK would be glad to explore it together.

Students who want to join a future edition can also register their interest through KBI HK and be contacted when a suitable cohort opens.
