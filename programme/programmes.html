ETO Eastern Conference 2026

Our centrepiece annual event — ETO Eastern Conference 2026 — is a global emerging-technologies conference at CDNIS, Hong Kong (10–11 October). Cross-disciplinary domains (BCTT, DSAI, QCIT); distinct from the I2OL subject olympiads.

Three Olympiads.
One Mission.

The KBI Journey

From Newcomer to Olympiad Finalist.

KBI HK's programmes are designed to form a coherent pathway — each stage building the skills and credibility needed for the next.

1
Join & Train
Become a member and attend training clinics in your chosen technology track to build foundational knowledge.
2
Get Certified
Complete the 6-session clinic programme and earn an ISFRETIC certification — a verified, portable credential.
3
Compete at ETO
Apply your skills in the Emerging Technologies Olympiad — KBI HK's flagship, multi-track competition.
4
Go International
Top performers qualify for the IBCOL, IDSOL, or IQCOL international rounds, competing on a global stage.
