About KBI Hong Kong

Our Team

KBI Hong Kong is run by volunteers — university students and recent graduates who organise programmes, events, and community activity across four functional teams.

How We Work

KBI HK Team Structure

Chapter work is organised into four teams. Each team owns a distinct part of how KBI Hong Kong runs — from long-range planning and partnerships to the logistics of competition days. Members typically serve on one primary team and collaborate across teams when programmes require it.

SPT Strategic Planning — direction, partnerships, KBI International coordination
COT Communications & Outreach — publicity, branding, recruitment, external comms
PPT Programmes & Projects — learning programmes, certification, participant development
EPT Event Production — logistics, hospitality, operations, on-site execution
