About KBI Hong Kong

Our Team

KBI Hong Kong is a youth-led volunteer chapter organised around a clear team structure. Different people contribute in different ways, but the goal is shared: to build serious programmes, events, and communities around emerging technologies.

Structure

How we work

KBI HK is organised into four core teams, supported by advisors. Each team owns a distinct part of how the chapter runs — from long-range planning and partnerships to programme design, communications, and on-the-ground execution.

Members usually contribute through one primary team while collaborating across others when a project or event requires it. That structure helps us stay flexible without losing accountability.

Teams

Team structure

SPT

Strategic Planning Team (SPT)

The Strategic Planning Team looks after direction, partnerships, and coordination across the wider KBI ecosystem. This is where longer-term thinking happens: chapter priorities, institutional relationships, and how KBI HK fits into the broader mission.

COT

Communications & Outreach (COT)

The Communications & Outreach team is responsible for how KBI HK presents itself publicly. This includes publicity, branding, recruitment, outreach, and the external communications that help people understand who we are and why our work matters.

PPT

Programmes & Projects (PPT)

The Programmes & Projects team supports the learning side of KBI HK: programmes, workshops, certifications, and participant development. It is where ideas become pathways that people can actually join and grow through.

EPT

Event Production (EPT)

The Event Production team handles logistics, hospitality, operations, and on-site execution. From planning competition days to making sure people feel welcomed in the room, this team turns plans into experiences.

ADV

Advisors

Advisors provide continuity, perspective, and senior guidance. They support the chapter’s development without replacing the leadership and initiative of the volunteer team itself.

Working style

What this means in practice

KBI HK is not run by one person doing everything alone. It works because responsibility is distributed across teams, and because volunteers step in where they are strongest — strategically, creatively, technically, or operationally.

Some people help shape direction. Some build programmes. Some communicate the work. Some make events actually happen. Together, that is what keeps the chapter moving.

Joining

Interested in contributing?

Not everyone joins KBI HK in the same way. Some begin as participants in competitions or events; some join through membership and later volunteer; some contribute because they care about building a serious emerging-technology community in Hong Kong.

If that sounds like something you want to be part of, membership is usually the best place to start.
